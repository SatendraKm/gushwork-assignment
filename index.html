Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of a laser cutter for sheet metal?

It is designed to cut various types of sheet metal with precision, allowing for intricate designs and shapes that are essential in manufacturing processes.

What are the benefits of using aluminium tubing in manufacturing?

Aluminium tubing offers excellent strength-to-weight ratio, corrosion resistance, and is highly recyclable, making it ideal for manufacturing applications.

How is aluminium tubing produced?

Aluminium tubing is produced through extrusion processes where aluminium billets are heated and forced through dies to create the desired profile.

What are the common applications of aluminium tubing?

Common applications include aerospace, automotive, construction, sporting goods, and industrial equipment manufacturing.

Can aluminium tubing be customized?

Yes, aluminium tubing can be customized in terms of diameter, wall thickness, alloy composition, and surface finish to meet specific application requirements.
